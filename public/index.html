Your Earnings

Two Powerful Ways to Earn

TechGeo combines task-based income with a network commission structure so you can earn even while offline.

👥

Referral Commissions

Every time someone you refer — or someone in your extended network — activates their account, you earn a commission. This applies across 4 levels.

  • Level 1 — KES 200 per activation (your direct referrals)
  • Level 2 — KES 150 per activation (referrals of your referrals)
  • Level 3 — KES 75 per activation (third level down)
  • Level 4 — KES 25 per activation (fourth level down)
  • Commissions are automatic and paid to your KES wallet instantly
📝

Task Earnings

Complete digital tasks posted on the platform and earn points for each approved submission. Points accumulate in your wallet and can be redeemed on withdrawal day.

  • Blog writing tasks — earn points per approved article
  • Survey tasks — earn points per completed survey
  • Transcription tasks — earn points for typing audio content
  • Writing jobs — earn points for submitted written work
  • Data entry tasks — earn points for completed data files
4-Level Network

Earn Across Four Levels

When someone in your network activates, you earn. This includes not just the people you invite directly, but also the people they invite, and so on — four levels deep.

Level 1
KES 200
Per activation from people you personally invited
Level 2
KES 150
Per activation from people your referrals invited
Level 3
KES 75
Per activation from the third level of your network
Level 4
KES 25
Per activation from the fourth level of your network

Example: You invite 5 people. Each of them invites 5 more. Each of those invites 5 more. That is 5 direct commissions at KES 200, 25 at KES 150, and 125 at KES 75 — all credited to your account automatically as each person activates.

Withdrawals

Regular, Structured Payouts

TechGeo processes withdrawals on fixed days each week to ensure reliable and orderly payments to all members.

Tuesday — Task Earnings

Redeem your accumulated task points for KES every Tuesday. You must have reached the minimum points threshold and have a qualifying number of new referrals since your last redemption.

Thursday — Referral Commissions

Withdraw your referral commission earnings every Thursday. Commissions accumulate in your KES wallet as your network grows. A minimum withdrawal amount applies.

Payment Methods

All payments are made via M-Pesa or direct bank transfer. You provide your payment details when submitting a withdrawal request. No delays — processed same day.
