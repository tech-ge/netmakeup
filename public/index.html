Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I get a referral code to join? ➕

You need a referral code from an existing member. Ask friends or find promoters on our social media pages. The code is required during registration.

When can I withdraw my earnings? ➕

Withdrawals are processed every Thursday only. Minimum withdrawal is KES 300.

How are referral commissions calculated? ➕

4-Level system: Level 1: KES 200, Level 2: KES 150, Level 3: KES 75, Level 4: KES 25 per active referral in your downline.

What happens if my blog or survey is rejected? ➕

You can do others and submit. Make sure your content is original, meets word count, and follows guidelines. Quality submissions get approved faster.

Is the KES 700 activation fee refundable? ➕

The activation fee is a one-time payment to activate your account and start earning. It is non-refundable but you earn it back through your first referrals.
